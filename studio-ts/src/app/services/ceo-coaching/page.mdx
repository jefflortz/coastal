## Overview

Running a privately held business is a fundamentally isolated experience.

You have employees, advisors, maybe a board — but nobody in the room who has actually sat in your seat, managed through a tough quarter, made a hard call about a key hire, or figured out how to step back without the business falling apart.

Owner & CEO Coaching is a monthly 1:1 engagement built around the decisions and challenges that are actually in front of you. Not a curriculum. Not a framework walked through in sequence. Real work on real problems, with a coach who has been in the seat.

Jeff is a certified FocalPoint Executive and Business Coach — trained in one of the most established methodologies in the field. More importantly, he's a former PE-backed CEO and C-suite operator who has lived the challenges his clients bring to every session.
